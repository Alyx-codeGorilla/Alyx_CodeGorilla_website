Who Am I?

Hi there! My name is Marian and I am 29 years old. I was born in Malaysia, Miri, where I have lived the first year of my life.
Miri is a coastal city in north-eastern Sarawak, Malaysia, bordering with the forest of Borneo. If you love fresh coconuts, seafood and the beach, visit here!
After a year my dad said, "that's enough"; we thus moved to the small town of Assen where I spent my childhood.
