Food

I enjoy food, and I've come to realize that I'm really drawn to Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine. The flavors are just so rich and satisfying.

When it comes to dessert, I have a bit of a sweet tooth. Okay, let's be honest, I can't resist anything sweet! Cakes, pies, baklava, you name it. I just love indulging in something delicious after a meal.
